About Us

Power System Experts is a power system engineering consultancy specializing in modelling, simulation, and analysis. A team of 10 power system engineers with extensive experience, including extensive design of utility-scale renewable-generating systems. Our team has global exposure and have worked on many projects collectively within USA, Canada, Australia, South East Asia, the Middle East and Africa.

Our Mission

Our Mission is enable our clients for establishing safer, reliable and cleaner power systems across the planet by providing them with expert advice, capacity building and innovative ways of problem solving.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a globally recognized power system solutions provider.

What we can do for You

Power System Experts (PSE) provides a wide range of services for both static and dynamic power system analysis and connection studies. We can assist in operational performance and future planning, determine optimum operating configurations, and compare alternatives to support capital projects operations and expenditures. Our expertise is in providing innovative, reliable and cost-effective engineering services that exceed the client's expectations. We offer the most up-to-date, timely solutions and power system analysis methodologies available to our clients. We work across various industry sectors, providing power grid solutions to the sectors associated with mining, oil and gas, renewable energies, and utility transmission and distribution providers.
